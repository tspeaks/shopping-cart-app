Welcome to MyShoppingPortal!

This app serves as a demonstration of MyShoppingPortal once it is integrated with your merchandise store.

Please Sign-up as a user and get familiar with how your customers will view and interact with your merchandise.

Users can Signup and Login into their dashboard to view sample merchandise for purchase.< If at any time you would like to delete a user to start over fresh, simply delete the user from the dashboard page and sign up with a new user.

Thank you for taking the time to get comfortable with MyShoppingPortal.
